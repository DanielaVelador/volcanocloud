Our commitment

Volcano Cloud is a community dedicated to empowering people through learning and collaborating around technology. Its goal is to provide its members with the tools, skills, and knowledge necessary to take full advantage of technological opportunities and become leaders in their respective fields. Volcano Cloud members are passionate about technology and are committed to sharing their knowledge and experience to help others grow and develop in the technology space. The community offers a wide range of events and resources, including talks, workshops, hackathons, discussion forums, and more, so that members can learn and collaborate in a supportive and respectful environment.


Our environment

The following behaviors include, but are not limited to, maintaining a positive and respectful community environment:

  • Respect all members and avoid discriminating.
  • Be an active listener and foster an atmosphere of constructive dialogue.
  • Respect each community member's privacy and avoid disclosing personal information without consent.
  • Be aware of how our words and actions affect others.
  • Constructively participate in discussions and debates and contribute enriching ideas to promote teamwork.
  • Report any inappropriate behavior to community leaders.
  • Help other community members by providing support and assistance.
  • Recognize that all community members have different levels of knowledge and skills.
  • Deal with any disagreements or conflicts in a constructive and respectful manner.

    • Behavior that does not maintain a positive and respectful community environment will not be tolerated. Negative behaviors include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • Any form of verbal, physical or online harassment, including making offensive comments, intimidating, or defaming any member of the community.
  • Acts of discrimination based on race, gender, sexual orientation, religion, disability, age, or any other characteristic.
  • Posting, sharing, or distributing content that is offensive, violent, pornographic and/or defamatory within the community.
  • Disclose confidential personal information about other members without their consent, whether in a public or private setting.
  • Use lewd comments, make sexual advances, or use any other sexually inappropriate language or behavior.


    • Our responsibility

    We, as the leaders of Volcano Cloud, are responsible for making other members of the community aware of this Code of Conduct, ensuring that it is followed, and acting in cases of inappropriate, offensive, or harmful behavior as described above to determine whether to issue a warning, temporary suspension, or permanent ban to those involved in violating this Code of Conduct. We also have a responsibility to clearly communicate to all members when changes are made to the Code of Conduct.


    Scope

    This Code of Conduct applies to all members of the Volcano Cloud community, including organizers, attendees, and guest speakers, as well as to all events, activities, presentations, conferences, workshops, talks, hackathons, and discussion forums related to the community, both online and offline. It also applies to anyone officially representing Volcano Cloud in any public or private setting. You represent Volcano Cloud when you participate in community events, use our official communication channels to publish content, and/or explicitly have a representative role in any type of event.


    Incident report

    If you experience any form of harassment, observe negative behavior toward others, or have any other concerns, please contact us immediately by emailing mlsa-colima@outlook.com. Our team will be happy to assist you with anything you need, including maintaining your anonymity if you wish, providing chaperones, and making sure you feel safe during the event. We expect all members of our community to follow these rules at all Volcano Cloud-related activities and events. We are committed to working together to ensure an inclusive, positive, safe, and respectful environment for all participants. As a community, we are committed to continuing to work together to maintain an environment where all members feel valued, respected, and a part of the community. Respect for one another and each member's contribution to creating an inclusive and safe environment are critical to the growth of our community.
